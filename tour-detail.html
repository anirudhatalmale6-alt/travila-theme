About This Tour

Embark on an unforgettable 5-day journey through the heart of Bali, Indonesia. This carefully curated tour takes you through ancient temples, stunning rice terraces, and pristine beaches while immersing you in the rich Balinese culture.

From the iconic Tegallalang Rice Terraces to the mystical Tirta Empul Temple, every day brings new discoveries and breathtaking moments. Enjoy authentic local cuisine, traditional dance performances, and the warm hospitality that makes Bali truly magical.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the iconic Tegallalang Rice Terraces at sunrise
  • Explore ancient Tirta Empul & Uluwatu Temples
  • Traditional Balinese cooking class with local chef
  • Snorkeling trip to Nusa Penida crystal waters
  • Private sunset dinner on Jimbaran Beach
  • Professional English-speaking guide throughout

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Day 1 — Arrival & Ubud Exploration

Airport pickup and transfer to your luxury villa in Ubud. Afternoon visit to Ubud Monkey Forest and Royal Palace. Welcome dinner at a traditional Balinese restaurant.

Luxury Villa Dinner included
2

Day 2 — Rice Terraces & Water Temple

Early morning visit to Tegallalang Rice Terraces for the best photos. Proceed to Tirta Empul Temple for the holy water purification experience. Afternoon Balinese cooking class.

Luxury Villa All meals
3

Day 3 — Nusa Penida Island Adventure

Full day trip to Nusa Penida. Visit Kelingking Beach, Angel's Billabong, and Broken Beach. Snorkeling with manta rays at Manta Point. Return to mainland.

Beach Resort Lunch included
4

Day 4 — Uluwatu Temple & Kecak Dance

Relaxed morning. Afternoon visit to the clifftop Uluwatu Temple for the iconic sunset Kecak fire dance performance. Private sunset dinner on Jimbaran Beach.

Beach Resort Dinner included
5

Day 5 — Departure

Free morning for last-minute shopping at Ubud Art Market. Transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport for your departure flight.

Breakfast included
